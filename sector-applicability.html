Legal & Compliance

Legal AI deployments carry professional liability implications. Agent outputs must be structurally classified as informational — never legal advice.

Contract review, clause extraction, obligation identification, and M&A due diligence support.

Attorney review gate required. Output classification enforced structurally. Privilege logging per matter.
