Sector 02 — Detail Page

Legal & Compliance

Governance framework applied to the Legal & Compliance sector, including risk profile, agent deployment patterns, structural controls, and compliance considerations.

⚑
Primary Risk Vector
Agent-generated legal analysis presented as authoritative without professional qualification or attorney review

Agent Use Cases

Contract Review

Agent performing automated clause extraction, obligation identification, and risk flag classification across large contract sets for attorney review.

Due Diligence Support

Agent processing document volumes for M&A transactions, surfacing material issues and inconsistencies for legal team assessment.

Jurisdiction Compliance Checking

Agent checking draft contracts against maintained regulatory databases for jurisdiction-specific requirements — outputs classified as informational only.

Governance Controls Required

  • ✓Output classification enforced structurally — informational vs. advisory applied before any output exits the system
  • ✓Attorney review gate required before output enters client-facing systems
  • ✓Attorney-client privilege logging for all document interactions
  • ✓Jurisdiction and governing law parameters locked per session configuration
  • ✓Document chain-of-custody logging per matter

Logging & Audit Expectations

  • ◆Per-matter AI cost attribution for billing transparency
  • ◆Document chain-of-custody log per matter
  • ◆Model confidence scores recorded alongside all outputs
  • ◆Attorney review events: reviewer, decision, timestamp

Compliance & Oversight Framework

Unauthorised practice of law risk requires governance layer to enforce human attorney in all client-advisory pathways. Model confidence scores must be surfaced explicitly in all outputs. Every document interaction logged for privilege chain-of-custody. Per-matter AI cost attribution for client billing transparency.
